Abstract. In 1995, Ismail and Masson introduced orthogonal polynomials of types RI and
RII, which are deпђБned by speciпђБc three-term recurrence relations with additional conditions.
Recently, Kim and Stanton found a combinatorial interpretation for the moments of orthogonal
polynomials of type RI in the spirit of the combinatorial theory of orthogonal polynomials due
to Flajolet and Viennot. In this paper, we push this combinatorial model further to orthogo-
nal polynomials of type RII. Moreover, we generalize orthogonal polynomials of type RII by
relaxing some of their conditions. We then prove a master theorem, which generalizes combi-
natorial models for moments of various types of orthogonal polynomials: classical orthogonal
polynomials, Laurent biorthogonal polynomials, and orthogonal polynomials of types RI and
RII.
